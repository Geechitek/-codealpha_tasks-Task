## Afrobite — Low Fidelity Wireframes

Low fidelity wireframes created as part of the Afrobite food delivery
app UI design project, developed during the CodeAlpha internship program.



 ## About These Wireframes

These are the low fidelity (lo-fi) wireframes that served as the
structural blueprint for the Afrobite mobile app design.

They were created before the high fidelity screens to:
- Map out the layout and structure of each screen
- Plan the user flow from onboarding to order placement
- Focus on functionality and placement without visual distractions
- Gather early feedback before committing to a full design



##  App Concept

Afrobite is a food delivery mobile app designed for university
and college students who are too tired to cook after lectures
and need quick access to nearby local restaurants.

### Onboarding
- Welcome screen

### Core App Screens

##  Wireframe Details

- **Fidelity level:** Low fidelity
- **Design tool:** Figma
- **Export format:** PNG at 2x resolution
- **Style:** Grayscale, basic shapes, placeholder text and images
- **Purpose:** Layout planning and early user flow mapping
