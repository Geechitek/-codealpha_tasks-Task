# 🍛 Afrobite — Food Delivery App UI Design

A mobile UI design project created as part of the CodeAlpha internship program.
Designed in Figma, built around the real needs of students who want
quick and easy access to good local food after a long day of lectures.


## 📱 About Afrobite

Afrobite is a food delivery mobile app designed for students
who are too tired to cook after lectures and just want a
good meal without the stress.

With Afrobite, students can:
- Search for local restaurants near their campus
- Browse menus and discover new local spots
- Order their favourite meals quickly and easily
- Track their order from restaurant to door

The design focuses on simplicity, speed, and a warm African food culture aesthetic
making it feel familiar and welcoming to the target user.



## 🎯 Target Audience

University and college students who:
- Have little time or energy to cook after lectures
- Want affordable, nearby food options
- Are comfortable ordering food through their phones
